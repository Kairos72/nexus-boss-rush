# Nexus: Boss Rush - Project Vision

## Executive Summary

**Nexus: Boss Rush** is a premium mobile roguelike that brings FFXIV-style raid mechanics to your pocket. Players face 10 unique bosses in 30-minute skill-based runs with no grinding, no gacha, and no predatory monetization.

**Core Promise:** "FFXIV meets Hades on mobile. Pure skill, pure action, pure respect."

## Design Pillars

### 1. Skill-Based Mastery
- Player improvement IS the progression
- Every death teaches, every victory earns
- No gear carrying you, no level advantages
- Frame-perfect execution rewarded

### 2. Respect Player Time
- 30-minute runs, perfect for mobile
- No grinding required
- No energy systems or timegates
- Quality over quantity

### 3. Premium Quality, Premium Pricing
- No predatory monetization
- Complete game experience
- Transparent pricing
- Polish and performance first

## Target Market

**Primary: "The Lapsed Raider" (60% of market)**
- Age: 25-40
- Former FFXIV/WoW raiders with limited time now
- Want skill-based challenges without time commitment
- Willing to pay premium for quality

**Secondary: "The Mobile Enthusiast" (30% of market)**
- Age: 20-35
- Mobile-first gamers seeking depth
- Tired of predatory F2P mechanics

## Business Model

- **Free Demo**: 3 bosses, 1 class, Normal difficulty
- **Premium Unlock**: $12.99 one-time purchase
  - 10 bosses, 3 classes, all difficulties
  - 50-100+ hours of content
- **Post-Launch DLC**: Optional $4.99 expansions

## Success Metrics

**Year 1 Targets:**
- Free downloads: 100-150K
- Conversion rate: 20-30%
- Revenue: $260K-585K
- App Store Rating: 4.5+ stars

## Competitive Advantages

1. **First-Mover in Niche**: Only game combining FFXIV raids + roguelike + boss rush
2. **AI-Accelerated Development**: Ship faster than traditional studios
3. **Mobile-First Design**: Not a PC port
4. **Anti-Predatory Positioning**: Strong brand differentiation
5. **Premium Quality**: No compromises on gameplay
